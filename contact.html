Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from visitors and new members

What should I expect on my first visit?

Expect a warm, joyful atmosphere focused on Jesus and who you are in Him. Services include heartfelt worship, practical teaching, and a loving community ready to welcome you.

How long are your services?

Sunday services last about two hours with vibrant praise, a powerful message, and time to connect afterwards.

Do you have programs for children and teenagers?

Yes, LOGIC KidZone serves children while Teens Church ministers to teenagers with age-appropriate, engaging teaching.

What does LOGIC stand for?

LOGIC means Love Of God In Christ. Every expression of our ministry flows from the revelation of God‚Äôs love in Jesus.

How can I get connected?

Join a GIST Centre, serve in a department, or participate in outreach. Speak with a team member or connect online to find your next step.

Is there follow-up after my first visit?

Absolutely. Our follow-up officers reach out to pray with you, answer questions, and help you settle into the LOGIC family.

Is parking available?

Yes. We offer free on-site parking with clearly marked spaces, including accessible spots close to the entrance.

What do you believe as a church?

We are a grace-based, Christ-centered church. Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus alone, and believers are righteous in Christ, empowered to live victorious lives above sin.

How do I become a member of LOGIC Church?

Start by attending services, join a GIST Centre, and complete our Foundation Class for your growth and to understand who we are as a church, our mission and values.

Do you stream your services online?

Yes. Our services are streamed online so you can worship and receive the Word from anywhere in the world.
